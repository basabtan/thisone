Najd Fault System — Slope Orientation Analysis

Circular statistics & bimodal population analysis · 5 sites · ~500 dip-direction measurements

Granodiorite slopes · Saudi Arabia
Headline: 4 of 5 sites show statistically significant directional clustering (Rayleigh test, p < 0.05), meaning slopes are structurally controlled rather than randomly oriented. Site 3 has the strongest concentration (R = 0.735); Site 5 is statistically uniform (R = 0.140). Bimodal analysis suggests Najd-aligned (NW–SE) and Red Sea-aligned (NE–SW) populations coexist in varying ratios across sites.
