About this demo

This is a stand-in for one of your content pages (here, najd-roadmap.html). The bottom strip is the real navigation tool — sharp editorial cards in the pearl finish, one per destination, numbered like a journal contents index.

The current page (Roadmap) is shown as inactive with a full-width gold rule, so you always know where you are. Hover the others to feel the lift and the gold rule extend; the index number turns gold on hover too.

Try it: hover any card · click the ✦ disc at the right to open the nav-style popover · resize the window narrow to see it collapse into a scrollable rail. On your real pages the strip auto-hides on index.html (welcome) and the KB hub.
